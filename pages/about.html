About Pedal Faster

March 2025

Welcome to Pedal Faster, the premier magazine for modern bicycle enthusiasts. Our passion for cycling drives us to explore new trends, review innovative gear, and share stories from the cycling community worldwide.

Founded by a group of dedicated cyclists, our mission is to provide insightful articles on urban commuting, bike technology, and sustainable transportation. Whether you're a fixed-gear fanatic, a long-distance tourer, or an everyday commuter, we've got something for you.

What We Cover

  • Latest bike technology and gear reviews
  • Urban cycling tips and city guides
  • Exclusive interviews with cycling pioneers
  • Stories from passionate riders around the globe
