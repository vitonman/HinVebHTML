Types of Bicycles

Bikes come in many styles, each suited for different needs:

  • Road Bikes: Built for speed on paved roads, lightweight and aerodynamic.
  • Mountain Bikes: Designed for rough terrain, with suspension and durable frames.
  • Hybrid Bikes: A mix of road and mountain bikes, great for urban commuting.
  • Fixed Gear Bikes: Simple and stylish, popular among urban cyclists.
